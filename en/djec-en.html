Children’s dental practice and preventive dentistry

Children are very special patient to us, and gaining their trust is complex proccess. However, once achieved, this relationship is important for the health of your child’s teeth as for the organism itself.
In our practice, we devote ourselves to create a bound with noncooperative children or children with unpleasant previous experience.
Prevention is especially important for children, which suppose regular check-ups as well as dental sealants as the best prevention of dental decay (we use high tech materials).
All of this is to be done with the minimal invasive dentistry to maximally protect both, baby and permanent teeth.
Besides prevention and cavity treatment, we also offer prevention and therapy of other orthodontic anomalies.
