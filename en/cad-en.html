Cad/Cam

COMPUTER AIDED DESIGN / COMPUTER AIDED MANUFACTURING CAD/CAM technology is the new golden standard high tech dentistry has to offer. It makes us more improved, efficient, and faster. Zirconium oxide, diamond structure is the material, which is very hard, beautiful, and non-allergenic and provides perfect effects. Aesthetic dentistry nowadays uses zircon for crowns instead of metal. Compared with metal, zircon is biocompatible, which means, it does not causes any allergic reactions when applied to the tissue. Its adventage over the metal is that it is white colour (as teeth), so from the aesthetic point of view, it looks better. The aesthetic difference between non-metal and metal crown is that metal crowns do not absorp light as the zircon, so they do not have a natural look. Zircon crowns are special by the computer technology used in their construction ad they possess high quality.
