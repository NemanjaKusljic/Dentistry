About us

Dental practice Krsmanovic was founded in 2009 in Banjaluka. Our mission is to offer you “everything at one place”. Our highly skilled team provides a full range of dental treatments. What makes us special is our professional team as well as our high tech equipment.

Meet Your Doctors

dr Vanja Krsmanović

Graduated from the faculty of dentistry at the University of Belgrade. She is a resident in the field of oral surgery. Has 15 years of experience in aesthetic dentistry and prosthodontics. She also does medical applications of botox, PRP and lip fillers. Her primary interests are oral surgery, conservative and preventive dentistry. She continuously visits dental seminars in order to perfect her work in all fields of dentistry.

dr Snježana Radić

Graduated from the faculty of dentistry at the University of Banja Luka. Her primary interest is conservative dentistry, but she also practises aesthetic and reconstructive dentistry. She is a member of the Dental Guild RS and continuously visits dental seminars in order to perfect her work in all fields of dentistry.
